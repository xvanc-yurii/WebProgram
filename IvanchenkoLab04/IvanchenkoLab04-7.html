Google Apps Glossary

Gmail
A free email service developed by Google.
Google Drive
A cloud storage and file sharing service developed by Google.
Google Calendar
A time-management and scheduling calendar service developed by Google.
Google Docs
A cloud-based word processing service developed by Google.
Google Sheets
A cloud-based spreadsheet service developed by Google.
Google Slides
A cloud-based presentation service developed by Google.
Google Meet
A video conferencing and communication service developed by Google.
Google Forms
A cloud-based form creation and survey service developed by Google.
Google Sites
A cloud-based website creation and publishing service developed by Google.
Google Classroom
A cloud-based education platform developed by Google.
